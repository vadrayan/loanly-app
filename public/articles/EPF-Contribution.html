Employee PF contribution is taxable in new regime

Employee PF contribution is taxable in new regime. Old regime gave benefit of 80C. However new regime does not have any such benefit of 80C ,making your epf contribution taxable

Example Calculation

Let’s assume:

  • Annual CTC : ₹20,00000
  • EPF contribution ₹ 150000

While in old regime employee epf contribution of ₹ 150000 could be deducted from his annual 80C making taxable income as ₹18,50000, no such facility is available in new regime and your taxable income would still be ₹20,00000. However, epf is still a very good investment ,as it enjoys higher interest rate of 8.25% and interest on annual contribution till Rs 250000 is totally tax free.
