Home loan is cheapest and best loan

  • Home loan is the cheapest and best loan in India. It offers wide tenure , lower interest rates and security of making home.
  • While personal loans and car loans maximum tenure is 7-8 years and higher interest rate, home loans are offered at around 7-8 % based on credit rating.
  • Home loans provide best paying plans where most banks provide options of part prepayments which directly debit from principal amount.
  • Home loans emi can also be changed as required with adjustments in tenure after requesting banks.
  • Home loans offer better roi also. It provides security, and place to live.
  • When not required can be sold also at good price, rented as well to pay emi.
  • In big cities a decent 2bhk can easily provide 20k-40k per month in rent based on location and area development.
  • Home loan provides tax benefits as well in old tax regime where amount of Rs 2lakh can be claimed as tax deduction from gross salary.
